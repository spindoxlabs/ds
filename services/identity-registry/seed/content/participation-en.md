# Dataspace Participation Agreement

Version 1.0 — effective 2026-09-01

This agreement governs an organisation's participation in the dataspace as a
data consumer and/or data provider.

## 1. Capacity

The participating organisation acts as a **processor** on behalf of the data
controller for the purposes declared in the sharing offers it is admitted to.
It processes data only on documented instructions, may not use the data for its
own independent purposes, and is bound by the confidentiality and security
obligations set out below.

## 2. Obligations

- Process personal data only for the declared and consented purposes.
- Maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures.
- Assist the controller in meeting data-subject rights requests.
- Notify the controller without undue delay of any personal-data breach.

## 3. Credentials and revocation

Participation is evidenced by an OrganizationCredential issued by the dataspace
trust anchor. The credential may be suspended or revoked, immediately ending the
organisation's ability to negotiate contracts and pull data.

## 4. Term

This agreement remains in force for as long as the organisation holds a valid,
unrevoked OrganizationCredential and a current accepted version of this
agreement.
